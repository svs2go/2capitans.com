Discover the Route

Palma de Mallorca

Our adventure sets sail from the majestic Port of Palma. As we glide away, the iconic Cathedral provides a breathless backdrop—a city of stone meeting the infinite blue. This is where your luxury escape truly begins.

Departure Point

Cala Portals Vells

A sanctuary of serenity. We drop anchor in these shimmering turquoise waters to explore hidden sea caves and enjoy our first crystal-clear swim. The limestone cliffs and pine-scented air create a perfect Mediterranean morning.

12nm | 1.3 - 2h (at 6-9 kn)

Port d'Andratx

Entering this natural harbor feels like stepping into a postcard. Surrounded by dramatic peaks and high-end villas, d'Andratx is Mallorca's most exclusive corner. We'll enjoy sunset cocktails on the flybridge as the harbor lights begin to twinkle.

8nm | 0.9 - 1.3h (at 6-9 kn)

Port de Sóller

The wild West Coast reveals its crown jewel. A horseshoe bay protected by mountains where the historic orange tram still rattles along the shore. The view from the sea looking into this ancient port is nothing short of magical.

19nm | 2.1 - 3.2h (at 6-9 kn)

Cala Agulla

Venture to the wild northeast. Cala Agulla is a vast, untouched bay of white sands and deep turquoise hues. Surrounded by protected forests, it offers a raw connection to Mallorca's natural beauty, accessible best by sea.

43nm | 4.7 - 7.1h (at 6-9 kn)

Porto Colom

Step back in time in this traditional fishing village. With its iconic lighthouse and colorful houses, Porto Colom offers authentic charm. It's the perfect spot to experience the "slow life" that Mallorca is famous for.

23nm | 2.5 - 3.8h (at 6-9 kn)

Cabrera National Park

An archipelago frozen in time. Only a limited number of boats can enter this protected paradise each day. We'll swim in the Blue Cave and hike to the castle for a view that spans the entire southern horizon. Pure, silent wilderness.

24nm | 2.7 - 4h (at 6-9 kn)

Return to Palma

The final leg brings us back to the capital through the southern bays. A time for reflection, final swims, and a celebratory night in Palma. The loop is complete, but the memories of the Spring Adventure will last a lifetime.

21nm | 2.3 - 3.5h (Final Leg)

Note: The final program of our journey will be adapted during the trip through a joint decision of the crew, taking into account weather conditions and the shared desires of the group.
